# Privacy Policy — Chameleon Prop Hunt **Last updated:** 2026-06-27 **Developer:** jon mateo **Contact:** miappsandgames@gmail.com This Privacy Policy explains how the mobile game **Chameleon Prop Hunt** ("the Game", "we", "us") handles information. Please read it before using the Game. ## Summary (plain language) - The Game does **not** collect, sell, or share your personal information. - All progress (level, XP, coins, stats, achievements, settings) is stored **only on your device**. - The Game works **fully offline** in single-player. - The optional **online multiplayer** is a direct, peer-to-peer connection between players who choose to connect; it shares only the network address needed to connect and in-game position data during a match. No accounts, no servers operated by us. ## Information we collect **We do not collect any personally identifiable information.** The Game has no login, no account system, and no analytics or advertising SDKs in this version. ### Data stored locally on your device The Game saves the following to local device storage so your progress persists between sessions: - Player level and experience (XP) - In-game coins - Best score, longest survival, and other gameplay statistics - Unlocked items and achievements - Game settings This data never leaves your device and is removed if you uninstall the Game or clear its data. ### Online multiplayer (optional) If you choose to use the optional **Online Multiplayer (Beta)** feature: - A direct network connection is established between your device and the host/other players you connect to. - During a match, your in-game character position and chosen role are transmitted to the other connected players so they can see you in the game world. - Your device's network (IP) address is necessarily visible to the host you connect to, as with any direct online connection. - We do not operate a server, do not log this data, and do not retain any of it. ## Permissions The Game requests only the permissions required to run, including network access for the optional multiplayer feature. It does not request access to your contacts, location, camera, microphone, photos, or files. ## Children's privacy The Game does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. No personal data is gathered by us at all. ## Third-party services This version of the Game contains **no third-party advertising, analytics, or tracking services**. If a future version adds ads or in-app purchases, this Policy will be updated and the relevant third-party providers (e.g., Google Play Billing, an ad network) and their data practices will be disclosed here before those features are released. ## Data security Because no personal data is transmitted to or stored by us, there is no personal data for us to secure on a server. Locally stored progress is protected by your device's standard application sandboxing. ## Changes to this policy We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes. Continued use of the Game after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised Policy. ## Contact Questions about this Privacy Policy can be sent to: **miappsandgames@gmail.com** miappsandgames@gmail.com
