Privacy Policy — Camouflage Prop Hunt

Last updated: 2026-07-02

Developer: bon mateo

Contact: miappsandgames@gmail.com

This Privacy Policy explains how the mobile game Camouflage Prop Hunt (“the Game”, “we”, “us”) handles information. Please read it before using the Game.

Summary (plain language)

  • The Game does not collect, sell, or share your personal information.
  • There are no accounts, no ads, no analytics, and no tracking.
  • All progress (level, XP, coins, stats, achievements, settings) is stored only on your device.
  • The Game works fully offline in single-player.
  • The optional online multiplayer passes match data (a nickname you choose, room name, role, and in-game positions) through a small relay server in real time only — nothing is saved, logged, or kept after the match.

Information we collect

We do not collect any personally identifiable information. The Game has no login, no account system, and no analytics or advertising SDKs.

Data stored locally on your device

The Game saves the following to local device storage so your progress persists between sessions:

  • Player level, experience (XP), and in-game coins
  • Best score, longest survival, and other gameplay statistics
  • Unlocked items and achievements
  • Game settings (volume, sensitivity, graphics quality, difficulty)
  • The optional multiplayer nickname you enter

This data never leaves your device (except the nickname, as described below) and is removed if you uninstall the Game or clear its data.

Online multiplayer (optional)

If you choose to use the Online Multiplayer feature:

  • Your device connects over an encrypted connection (TLS/WSS) to a small relay server operated by us, whose only job is to forward match messages between the players in the same room.
  • The following information is transmitted during a match only: the nickname you choose (it does not have to be your real name — please don't use one), the room name the host typed, your chosen role, your in-game position, and game events (hits, eliminations, the round clock).
  • Other players in your room can see your nickname and your character in the game world.
  • This data is processed ephemerally: it is forwarded in real time, held only in the server's memory while the room exists, and is not logged, stored, or shared. When the last player leaves a room, the room and all of its data disappear.
  • As with any internet connection, the relay server necessarily sees your device's IP address in order to deliver messages. We do not log, store, or use IP addresses.

Permissions

The Game requests only network access (INTERNET), which is used solely for the optional multiplayer feature. It does not request access to your contacts, location, camera, microphone, photos, or files.

Children's privacy

The Game is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. Multiplayer nicknames are free-form: we encourage all players — especially children — to choose a nickname that is not their real name. No profile, history, or other information is attached to a nickname, and it is discarded when the match ends.

Third-party services

This version of the Game contains no third-party advertising, analytics, or tracking services. The multiplayer relay runs on a commercial hosting provider (which, like any host, may keep standard infrastructure logs subject to its own privacy policy), but the Game itself sends it no personal data beyond what is described above. If a future version adds ads or in-app purchases, this Policy will be updated and the relevant providers disclosed here before those features are released.

Data retention and deletion

  • Local progress: stays on your device until you uninstall the Game or clear its data — that deletes everything.
  • Multiplayer data: exists only in server memory during a match and is deleted automatically the moment the room closes. There is nothing stored for us to delete on request, but you can contact us at the address below with any question or concern.

Data security

Multiplayer traffic is encrypted in transit (WSS/TLS). Because no personal data is stored by us, there is no personal data at rest on a server to secure. Locally stored progress is protected by your device's standard application sandboxing.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top will reflect any changes. Continued use of the Game after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised Policy.
